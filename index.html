Traffic Signal Rules

Here you can provide detailed information about the traffic rules related to the selected signal.

DO's for Red Signal
  • Stop Completely: Come to a complete halt before the stop line or crosswalk.
  • Be Aware of Others: Look for pedestrians, cyclists, or other vehicles before proceeding.
  • Yield to Pedestrians: Yield to pedestrians and other vehicles that have already entered the intersection.
  • Wait for the Green: Wait for the green signal or a clear path before proceeding.
DON'Ts for Red Signal
  • Don't Run the Red: Don't proceed through the intersection or cross the stop line.
  • Don't Speed Up:Don't speed up to try to make it through the intersection before the light turns red.
  • Don't Ignore the Signal:Don't ignore the red signal or try to "beat the light.
  • Don't Block the Intersection: Don't block the intersection or crosswalk with your vehicle.
