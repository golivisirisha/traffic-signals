Red Signal Rules
  • Don't Run the Red: Don't proceed through the intersection or cross the stop line.
  • Don't Speed Up:Don't speed up to try to make it through the intersection before the light turns red.
  • Don't Ignore the Signal:Don't ignore the red signal or try to "beat the light.
  • Don't Block the Intersection: Don't block the intersection or crosswalk with your vehicle.
  • Don't Use Mobile Phones: Don't use mobile phones or engage in distracting activities while driving or walking near a red signal..
DO's for Red Signal
  • Stop Completely: Come to a complete halt before the stop line or crosswalk.
  • Be Aware of Others: Look for pedestrians, cyclists, or other vehicles before proceeding.
  • Yield to Pedestrians: Yield to pedestrians and other vehicles that have already entered the intersection.
  • Wait for the Green: Wait for the green signal or a clear path before proceeding.
  • Follow Traffic Rules: Follow all traffic rules and signs, including pedestrian crossings and traffic signals.

Yellow signal rules
  • Caution Ahead: The yellow signal indicates that the red light is about to appear.
  • Slow Down: Slow down and prepare to come to a complete stop.
  • Don't Accelerate: Do not accelerate or try to beat the red light.
  • Stop Before the Line: Come to a complete stop before the stop line or crosswalk, if possible.
  • Use Common Sense: Use your judgment and common sense when approaching an intersection with a yellow signal.
DO's for Yellow Signal
  • Slow Down: Gradually slow down your vehicle to come to a safe stop.
  • Prepare to Stop: Get ready to stop by taking your foot off the accelerator and preparing to press the brake pedal.
  • Be Cautious: Be cautious of pedestrians, bicyclists, and other vehicles that may be in the intersection.
  • Use Your Judgment: Use your judgment to determine if you can safely stop before the intersection or if it's better to proceed with caution.
  • Check Your Surroundings: Check your surroundings, including your rearview and side mirrors, to make sure it's safe to stop.
DON'Ts for Yellow Signal
  • Don't Speed Up: Don't try to speed up to beat the red light.
  • Don't Run the Red: Don't run the red light, even if you think you can make it through the intersection before it turns red.
  • Don't Tailgate:Don't tailgate or follow other vehicles too closely, as this can increase your stopping distance
  • Don't Take Risks: Don't take risks by trying to squeeze through the intersection or make a last-minute turn.
  • Don't Get Distracted: Don't get distracted by your phone, GPS, or other passengers while approaching an intersection with a yellow signal.
